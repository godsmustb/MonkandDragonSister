"The Vow of Compassion"

Years of training under falling blossoms. The monk's staff had tasted shadow-blood, the sister's dragon roar had shaken heaven itself. From grief came resolve. From compassion, legend.

Shadowling, Frost Imp, Tide Wraith, Venom Oni — each fell in turn. And at the last, the Inferno Demon Lord spread his burning wings over the garden. Only when the sea answered fire did his magma heart cool to ash. The demon lord has fallen.

The garden is cleansed. All four dragon forms now flow freely — fire and ice, poison and rain. The path to mastery stretches ever onward.

The garden is now your sandbox. All forms unlocked.

"The Frozen Summit"

High above the clouds, where frost splits stone and breath turns to ice, the monk and his sister faced warlords clad in frozen iron. The Fire Dragon's roar melted their armour. The monk's chi shield shattered glaciers.

The Glacial Inferno Lord descended from the summit peak — ice-heart beating with ancient fire. When his frozen shell cracked open and magma poured forth, only the Water Dragon's tide could extinguish him. The peaks are silent. The snow falls clean.

The Glacial Peaks are freed. All dragon forms ring true — the bond between monk and sister grows stronger still.

All forms unlocked. The Venom Abyss awaits.

"The Venom Abyss"

In the blighted wastes below the peaks, where the air itself weeps poison, the monk and his sister descended into the Venom Abyss. Plague scouts, tide wraiths, and the dread Plague Oni — all fell to ice and chi. The ground itself dissolved beneath pools of ancient venom.

At the abyss's heart, the Abyssal Demon Lord rose — poison soul burning with tidal fury. When his venom heart shifted to churning water, only the Poison Dragon's breath could purify the tide. The abyss is still. The wastes begin to heal.
